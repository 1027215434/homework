Need some Adult Ultra Cotton T-Shirts for the ladies and kids?

We've got you covered with the Gildan 2000L and Gildan 2000B!

Some people get confused between the Ultra Cotton and the Heavy Cotton labels offered by GILDAN.

To clear things up: the Ultra Cotton is a bit heavier, weighing in at 10.1oz, while the Heavy Cotton is 8.9oz.

The difference is most noticeable when it comes to White.









The Gildan 2000 Adult Ultra Cotton T-Shirt is made from 10.1 oz. of 100% cotton jersey knit. This is Gildan's Ultra Cotton version of the classic 5000, featuring the same classic cut and style.
